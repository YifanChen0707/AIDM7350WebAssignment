We Want To Bring Young People Closer to Agriculture.

Shi Ge Qin Tian is a company dedicated to agriculture. The company has ten young members who work together to realize their agricultural dreams. The initial reason for establishing the company was that the ten members participated in a documentary reality show called Become a Farmer. In the show, the members worked hard for over 200 days, harvested 142.8 acres of rice, tended to 4000 pots of roses, raised 30 sheep and various poultry, planted wheat and various vegetables, farmed fish and shrimp, and adopted stray dogs. In order to legally sell their agricultural products, they registered and established an agricultural company named Shi Ge Qin Tian. Today, the company has been established for one year, and the ten members are still working hard, expanding their industry, and hoping to strengthen and expand Shi Ge Qin Tian. They believe in the power of the land and aim to change young people's biases towards agriculture through digital media, drawing more attention to the development of Chinese agriculture.
